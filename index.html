Who We Are - A Modern Day Gurukul

SYNERGY, a long-cherished dream of Shivani Educational and Charitable Trust, took shape in the year 1999. Located 70kms from the state capital Bhubaneswar on NH-55, it boasts excellent connectivity by road and rail. Blessed with a salubrious climate, SYNERGY is adorned with a historic temple and surrounded by a cluster of mines, mega steel, aluminium & power plants. With 21 years of glorious service to its credit, SYNERGY has achieved the rare distinction of Accreditation by NBA & NAAC (UGC). It is also Govt. of India approved by AICTE and affiliated to BPUT.

Synergy School of Engineering, a sister concern of Synergy Institute of Engineering and Technology, was established in 2017. The Institute has come a long way in imparting professional education in the field of Engineering & Technology. The journey has not been smooth, but   SYNERGY has steadily progressed. The college has secured approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Govt. of India. Additionally, the institute is affiliated with the State Council for Technical Education & Vocational Training, Odisha, and D.T.E.T, Odisha.
